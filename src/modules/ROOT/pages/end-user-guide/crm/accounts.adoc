= Accounts

The Accounts page is accessible by clicking on the "Accounts" in the main navigation bar on the top of the screen.

Accounts are entities (companies or other) that you are doing business with.
A contact is an employee that we are allowed to reach out to.

[NOTE]
====
The primary contact provides contact information when generating documents such as quotes.
====

[TIP]
====
You can create new contacts for the account by pressing the "Create new Contact from this account" button.
====

Accounts allow you to store information about your customers, such as company size, opportunities and business contact information.

The important action in an account is creating opportunities.
Press the "Create new Opportunity from this account" button to create a new opportunity.
