= Module permissions

== Any module

.To set permissions for any module:
. navigate to {PRODUCT_NAME} {APP_NAME_COMPOSE},
. enter the namespace administration area,
. navigate to the "Modules" subpage,
. click on the *lock icon* on top of the *module list*.

== Specific module

.To set permissions for a specific module:
. navigate to {PRODUCT_NAME} {APP_NAME_COMPOSE},
. enter the namespace administration area,
. navigate to the "Modules" subpage,
. click on the *lock icon* next to the module.

== Specific module field

.To set permissions for a specific module field:
. navigate to {PRODUCT_NAME} {APP_NAME_COMPOSE},
. enter the namespace administration area,
. navigate to the "Modules" subpage,
. click the edit icon next to the module,
. click on the *lock icon* next to a module field.
